Valentia Island Transatlantic Cable

As a contributor to the website, the Valentia Island Transatlantic Cable website is an extremely exciting project. The ultimate goal is to drive traffic to buy tours of the new state of the art Cable Station, the "Eigth Wonder of the World". The story of Valentia Island, which sits off the west coast of ireland, started over 150 years ago when the first transatlantic message was sent by cable from Europe to America. This reduced messaging time from weeks to mere minutes.

Vacation Rental Virtual Tours

Not a web project, but a wonderful use of Matterport technology to create virtual tours of vacation rentals and real estate listings.
